About This Project

CoastPulse Transit Atlas is a personal, unofficial transit information website focused first on the Gold Coast, with expanded Brisbane coverage, Logan preview coverage, and ongoing South East Queensland expansion scaffolding.

This website is not an official source and is not operated, sponsored, or endorsed by Translink or the Queensland Government.

Product intent

  • Fast map-first stop and route exploration
  • Clear upcoming departures with source transparency (live, sample, schedule)
  • Honest direct-only travel estimation with no fake transfer routing
  • Calm, high-clarity local transit UX for everyday use

Reliability notice

  • Data can be delayed, incomplete, stale, or approximate.
  • Live snapshots can fail and automatically fall back to sample/scheduled estimates.
  • Direct estimates are representative and may not match real operations at all times.
  • Always verify critical travel decisions with official channels before travel.

Scope boundaries

  • No full transfer planner, walking-transfer engine, or fare calculator.
  • No account system, login, admin portal, or backend runtime requirement.
  • No claim of official operational authority.

Local data and privacy

Saved stops, saved routes, and recent activity are stored in local browser storage only on your device. This static deployment does not provide cloud profile syncing.
