About This Project

CoastPulse Transit is a personal, unofficial public transit information website focused first on Gold Coast tram and major bus interchange nodes. It is built as a static-first GitHub Pages project so it can stay fast, low-cost, and easy to maintain.

This website is not an official source. It is not operated, sponsored, or endorsed by Translink or the Queensland Government.

Data Reliability Notice

  • Times can be delayed, incomplete, unavailable, or approximate.
  • Live feeds may fail or be stale and fall back to static/scheduled estimates.
  • Static direct-travel times are representative and may not match real-world operations at all times.
  • Always verify critical travel decisions with official channels before departing.

  • Simple direct line/route estimate only.
