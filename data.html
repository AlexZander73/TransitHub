Data and source notes

CoastPulse Transit Atlas is a static-first unofficial transit information website. It uses representative sample data in this repository, with optional live snapshots from either JSON feeds or GTFS-RT preprocessing when configured.

Current runtime data files

  • data/stops.json and data/interchanges.json for stop/node modeling
  • data/routes.json, data/lines.json, and data/route-patterns.json for route and map overlays
  • data/departures.sample.json and optional data/departures.live.json for departures
  • data/alerts.sample.json and optional data/alerts.live.json for alerts
  • data/direct-travel.sample.json for direct-estimate support edges
  • data/gtfs-id-map.sample.json template for provider-ID mapping in GTFS-RT ingestion

Important limitations

  • Not an official service and not affiliated with Translink or the Queensland Government.
  • Data may be delayed, incomplete, stale, or approximate.
  • Direct estimate logic supports only direct same-route journeys and does not provide full transfer planning.
  • Always verify critical travel decisions with official channels.

Privacy note

Favorites, recent stops, recent routes, and recent direct-trip checks are stored locally in your browser only. No account system or server-side profile storage is used in this static deployment.
